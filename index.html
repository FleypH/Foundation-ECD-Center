ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Foundation Pre-School is a registered early childhood development centre located in Rutenga, Mwenezi, in the Masvingo Province.

OUR JOURNEY

Foundation Pre-School is a registered early childhood development centre located in Rutenga, Mwenezi, in the Masvingo Province. It started in 2010.

Since opening our doors in 2010 with just 26 learners, we have experienced steady and inspiring growth.

By the end of our first term, enrolment had risen to 42 learners, and in the years that followed our numbers continued to increase, reaching between 70 and 90 learners.

Today, Foundation Pre-School proudly serves children from Rutenga and surrounding communities, providing a warm, safe, and high-quality learning environment.

Our dedicated team remains committed to delivering exceptional early childhood education, laying a strong foundation for every child's future.

PROGRAMS

ECD A, ECD B & Grade 1 where little hearts and big dreams grow together.

ECD A ages 3-4yrs

The warm, play-based first school experience where children feel safe and loved. Through free play, art, music, stories, outdoor fun, and simple themes, they learn to share, speak confidently, and build early skills.

ECD B ages 4-5yrs

The exciting getting ready for big school year. Through Jolly Phonics, pre-writing, counting to 20+, English, stories, art, music, themed projects and lots of outdoor play, children gain real skills while still having fun.

Grade 1 ages 5-6yrs

The confident bridge to primary school. Children follow the full Zimbabwe syllabus (ZIMSEC) with strong Jolly Phonics, daily reading and writing, Mathematics, Shona, Content, PE and Art in a structured yet caring environment.
